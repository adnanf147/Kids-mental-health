Most Popular Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and approach.

What age groups do you work with?

We provide mental health services for children and adolescents aged 3 to 18 years. Our therapists are trained in age-appropriate therapeutic approaches for different developmental stages.

How do I know if my child needs therapy?

Signs that your child might benefit from therapy include persistent sadness or anxiety, changes in eating or sleeping patterns, social withdrawal, academic decline, or behavioral changes. Our free consultation can help determine if therapy would be beneficial.

Is the AI assistant a replacement for therapy?

No, Kiko is designed as a supplemental tool to support children between therapy sessions. It provides emotional check-ins, coping strategies, and mindfulness exercises but is not a replacement for professional therapy with a licensed clinician.

How long does therapy typically last?

The duration of therapy varies based on the child's needs and goals. Some children benefit from short-term therapy (8-12 sessions), while others may engage in longer-term support. We regularly assess progress and adjust treatment plans accordingly.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. We also offer sliding scale fees based on income for families without insurance coverage. Contact our office for specific information about your insurance plan.
